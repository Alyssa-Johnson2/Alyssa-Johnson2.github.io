How It Began

The Johnsons



After arriving to Kellogg School of Management in June 2018, Alyssa had been trying to connect with her peers through brunches, coffees and other social efforts. Noticing how diligently and systematically Alyssa was trying to get to know her peers, Doug commented that it would be useful to have an app which could help her track and map her social networks more efficiently. And hence Konnect was born! Combined with her learnings about social networks and brokerage from an orientation course in Managing Organizations, Alyssa and Doug set out developing the concept and after putting new coding skills to work developing the first website, Alyssa brought the concept to life.
